Hotels on the Bluff or a guesthouse near Anstey’s Beach?

Published 6 July 2026 · 4Shore Guesthouse

If you’re searching for hotels on the Bluff, it’s worth comparing guesthouses too. A good guesthouse can give you the comfort you need, with a more personal, owner-run stay.

Quick answer: 4Shore Guesthouse isn’t a hotel. It’s an owner-run guesthouse at 28 Foreshore Drive, Ocean View, Bluff. For guests comparing hotels on the Bluff, it offers a B&B-style stay near Anstey’s Beach, with secure on-site parking, nine en-suite rooms, Wi-Fi, air-conditioning and direct contact with Winston, the owner and host.

The word hotel is often used in a search box when people simply mean accommodation. That’s understandable. You want somewhere clean, comfortable and easy to book. But the type of stay matters, especially on the Bluff.

A hotel can suit guests who want a larger building, a front desk and a more standard format. A guesthouse can suit guests who want fewer rooms, a direct person to speak to, and practical help before they arrive.

What a guesthouse gives you

A guesthouse is usually smaller than a hotel. The best ones feel personal without being intrusive. You should still expect clear booking details, a comfortable room, safe parking and honest information about what’s available.

4Shore Guesthouse has nine en-suite rooms with private entrances. Guests can ask Winston directly about current rates, room availability, parking and arrival details before they travel.

Why B&B-style works near the beach

A bed and breakfast-style stay works well when your plans are simple: sleep well, park safely, walk to the beach, and use the guesthouse as a base for Durban. It’s not about pretending a guesthouse is a hotel. It’s about explaining the experience clearly.

At 4Shore, the beach is part of the reason many guests look at the area. The guesthouse is across Foreshore Drive from Anstey’s Beach, which makes it practical for a short beach break, a family stay, a work trip or a longer coastal visit.

How to compare your options

Before you book any Bluff accommodation, check the details that will affect your stay. Is parking on site? Does the room suit your guest count? Is there Wi-Fi and air-conditioning? Can someone confirm your questions before you pay or travel?
