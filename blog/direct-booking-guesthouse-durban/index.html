Direct booking a Bluff guesthouse: what to ask before you confirm

Published 6 July 2026 · 4Shore Guesthouse

Direct booking is useful when you want clear answers before you travel. It helps you match the room, rate and arrival details to the stay you actually need.

Quick answer: When you book directly with a guesthouse, ask about the current rate, room fit, secure parking, Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, arrival details and beach access. At 4Shore Guesthouse, Winston, the owner and host of 4Shore Guesthouse, can confirm rooms, rates, availability and practical questions directly.

Many travellers start with a listing site, a map search or a quick hotel search. That’s fine for comparing the area, but it doesn’t always answer the small questions that matter once you arrive.

A direct enquiry gives you a human answer. It’s especially helpful for a smaller, owner-run place where the right room, parking plan and arrival time can make the stay easier from the start.

Ask what the rate includes

Before you confirm, ask for the current rate for your dates and guest count. Rates can change by room, date and demand, so use the site as a starting point and ask Winston to confirm the final rate directly.

If you’re comparing a B&B-style guesthouse with hotels on the Bluff, make sure you’re comparing the same things: room setup, parking, Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, beach location and how easy it is to contact someone before arrival.

Check the room setup before you pay

Tell the guesthouse how many people are travelling, whether you’re coming as a couple, family, solo guest or work traveller, and whether you have any practical preferences. 4Shore Guesthouse has nine en-suite rooms with private entrances, so it’s worth checking which room best suits your dates.

For family stays, ask how the room arrangement works. For business trips, ask about Wi-Fi, parking and arrival details. For a beach weekend, ask how easy it is to get to Anstey’s Beach and where to park when you return.

Confirm parking and arrival details

Secure on-site parking is one of the most useful things to check when booking Durban beach accommodation by car. Ask how parking works, where to enter, and what arrival time suits the guesthouse.
