Choose a base that keeps the weekend simple

For a Durban beach weekend, look for accommodation that lets you arrive, park, settle in, and get to the coast without a complicated plan. A beach stay works best when you can fit in a morning walk, a swim, a slow breakfast, or a quiet evening without turning every outing into a drive.

Check parking before you book

Secure parking is one of the first questions to ask, especially if you are arriving with luggage, surf gear, family bags, or a rental car. Ask where you park, whether it is on site, and what arrival details the host needs from you.

Match the room to your weekend

A couple’s weekend, a family beach break, and a solo reset do not all need the same setup. Before booking, confirm the current room options, the number of guests, rates, and whether the available room suits your plans.
