Choose accommodation near Anstey’s Beach by how you’ll use the beach

If you want one beach walk during a busy Durban trip, distance may not matter much. If you want beach time every day, it matters a lot.

Staying close means you can go out early, come back for a shower, rest between plans, or change your day when the weather shifts. That can make a short stay feel less rushed.

Check the room before you book

Don’t only look at the room photo. Ask what the room includes, how many people it suits, whether it has an en-suite bathroom, and whether the setup fits your travel style.

4Shore Guesthouse has nine en-suite guest rooms with private entrances. If you’re unsure which room to choose, contact Winston before booking. He’ll help you understand what is available for your dates.

Secure parking matters more than people think

Parking is not the exciting part of a beach stay, but it can affect the whole trip. If you’re travelling with luggage, work items, surf gear, or family bags, secure on-site parking is useful.

4Shore offers secure on-site parking. That helps if you plan to use your car for Durban outings and local trips around the Bluff.

Winston is the owner and host of 4Shore Guesthouse. That means you can ask him practical questions before you confirm, instead of guessing from a listing.
